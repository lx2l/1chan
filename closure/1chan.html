Closure

Hello 1chan faithful,

After many months of deliberation, I have arrived at the difficult decision that the site will remain closed indefinitely.

The legal structure (or lack thereof) of 1chan is such that as the owner of the site, I am personally accountable and liable for any abuse or illicit content that occurs. It has now come to the point that the risk and potential consequences are too much to bear.

Our moderation team has been exceptional and quick to respond when inappropriate content is posted, and I appreciate your efforts. I also appreciate the users who have been diligent in emailing or texting to alert me of spam when the moderators are not available.

Unfortunately the reality is that some of the content that has been spammed here in the past requires nothing less than immediate action and this is simply not feasible with a volunteer team. Sadly most of you know what I'm talking about.

I explored other options to address the situation, such as incorporating 1chan into an independent legal entity and engaging full time moderation team, but unfortunately such would be far too costly and time consuming to justify for a site with around a dozen peak active users. I can assure you that I do not want to shut down the site and would not be doing so if I were not fully convinced it is the only reasonable course of action.

By the way, I would like to apologize to those of you who emailed me recently and did not receive a response. Initially I did not want to comment on the behind the scenes situation until a decision has been made and that ended up taking a bit longer than I thought it would. Sorry about that. To those who offered donations of time or money, it is not necessary but I do appreciate the offer. Thanks.

Thank you to all the regulars. This is a great community and I am grateful to have been able to contribute to it in my small way. I hope the friendships formed here will continue long after the site is gone.

Your silent sysop,

thatdog
