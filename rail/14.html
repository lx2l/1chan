Yep, the D100 series diesels were created based on the design of F-M 38D8 1/8 (still not copied though). There were two series in mass production, 2000 HP atmospheric 2D100 and 3000 HP boosted 10D100. First came to TE3 and TE7 series locomotives and second to all machines of TE10 family.
Not a big secret that latest TE10 variants still are among the most popular diesel locomotive series across all Space-1520. But the problem with 10D100 is that after getting mechanical charger the diesel became if not to say extremely unreliable then at least extremely freakish. In practice it is almost impossible to make it work properly beyond a repair plant. Other than that they were just old so in early 2000-s TE10s began to massively pass overhaul with modernization during which this problematic diesel is replaced with stable proved 4-stroke V16 5D49 diesel. But there are thousands of these machines in service so they couldn't be remotorized all so there are at least a couple of hundreds of 10D100 TE10s in service on Russian railway network and a bit more in other countries of Space-1520.

Interesting fact, there were two base variants of such modernization. The one by Russian transport scientific-industrial complex and the one by... General Electric. There were certain comparative tests by the results of which was made a conclusion that Russian project is noticeably better by the price/quality ratio. Also it was stated that 5D49 has less fuel and oil consumption than GE 7FDL diesel. However GE project was liked by KTZh (Kazakhstan railways, do not confuse with KTZ, Kolomenskiy diesel locomotive plant or Kolomna diesel works, the manufacturer of D49 series) and https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yakutian_Railway so some of their 2TE10Ms were modernized by GE. By the way, the prototype of such modernization was remotorized with its own body and got fridge section only on one side (google 2TE10M-0881) but serial variants (2TE10MK-KN for KTZh and 2TE10MPGE for Yakutian Railway, maybe there are other differences than just name, however unsure) get Super Skid power modules.
